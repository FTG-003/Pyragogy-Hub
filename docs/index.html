Our Vision

About Pyragogy

Pyragogy represents a paradigm shift in education: a peer-driven, co-creative movement where humans and AI collaborate as equals to co-create transformative learning experiences and knowledge systems.

The Evolution of Learning:

Pedagogy

Traditional teacher-centered approach where knowledge flows one-way from teacher to student.

Andragogy

Adult learning theory that values learners' experience and encourages self-direction.

Heutagogy

Self-determined learning in which learners are highly autonomous, taking full charge of their own learning path.

Peeragogy

Peer-based learning where knowledge is co-created by learners as equals.

Pyragogy

Human-AI collaborative learning ecosystem that transcends traditional educational boundaries.

The Pyragogy Framework:

Human-AI Co-Creation

Collaborative generation of knowledge through a synergistic partnership between human and artificial intelligence.

Adaptive Learning Pathways

Personalized learning journeys that adapt in real time to feedback and evolving goals.

Cognitive Enhancement

Leveraging AI to augment human cognition and unlock new forms of understanding.

Ethical Framework

Ensuring a responsible and inclusive approach to AI in education, keeping human well-being at the center.

"Pyragogy isn't just a new educational method; it's a collective reimagining of how humans and AI learn together—creating something greater than either could achieve alone."
— The Pyragogy Community

The integration of AI into learning is not just inevitable—it's essential for education to evolve.

Community Catalyst

Fabrizio Terzi - (Bergamo|Hub)

Fabrizio Terzi is a pioneer in human-AI collaborative learning and one of the catalysts of the Pyragogy movement. His groundbreaking ideas, developed alongside a global community of collaborators, have helped lay the foundation for this new approach to education.

With a background spanning education, technology, and cognitive science, Fabrizio recognized early on that AI's true transformative potential is unlocked when it's not merely a tool, but a collaborative partner in the learning journey.

His research focuses on creating symbiotic educational environments where human creativity and AI capabilities amplify each other, leading to unprecedented knowledge creation and fostering both personal and collective growth. He prefers to work quietly behind the scenes to empower others to shine, ensuring that Pyragogy remains a truly community-driven effort.

Key Contributions

  • Developed the PeerGang System for AI-enhanced collaborative learning
  • Authored numerous papers on cognitive augmentation through AI partnership
  • Pioneered methodologies for adaptive human-AI feedback loops in education
